5-Axis Robotic Arm Design

Payload: ~100g Joint Spacing: ~20cm Stepper-based Feedback-ready

Designed a 5-axis robotic arm focusing on mechanical stability, repeatable motion, and reliable actuator control. Implemented stepper motor control with smooth acceleration/deceleration to reduce vibration and missed steps. The system architecture was planned to support sensors (endstops / homing) for improved accuracy.

Highlights:
• Mechanical trade-offs: torque per joint, arm reach vs payload, and cable management.
• Control: homing sequence, joint limits, and multi-axis trajectory planning.
• Reliability: power distribution planning and protection considerations.

DC Motor Torque Control System

Closed-loop PID Tuning Monitoring Protection

Developed a DC motor control system that maintains approximately constant torque while allowing adjustable speed. Used feedback signals to stabilize output under load variations with a PID-based control loop and PWM stage.

Highlights:
• Torque estimation using current sensing + filtering to reduce noise.
• Controller safety: limits + anti-windup considerations for stable response.
• Practical testing: step-load response, overshoot checks, and tuning for stability.

252 Battery Monitoring using PLC

252 Channels Limited Inputs Alarms Scalable

Engineered a scalable PLC-based monitoring solution to supervise 252 battery voltages using limited PLC inputs. Applied multiplexing/selection logic to scan battery groups, detect abnormal thresholds, and trigger alarms efficiently.

Highlights:
• Scanning strategy: grouped measurements + timing plan to avoid false alarms.
• Threshold logic: low-voltage/over-voltage alarms with debounce/hysteresis.
• Industrial readiness: maintainable channel mapping and wiring discipline.

PCB Design – Smart Power & Monitoring Board

Altium Designer Schematic + Layout ERC / DRC Gerber + BOM

Designed a fabrication-ready PCB in Altium Designer starting from schematic capture to final layout. Focused on stable power distribution, noise reduction, and clean routing practices for a reliable hardware system.

Highlights:

• Validation: footprint checks + ERC/DRC + manufacturing outputs (Gerber / NC Drill).
